Workouts

Incline Dumbbell Chest press

The incline dumbbell chest press targets the upper chest. Lie on an incline bench, press dumbbells up, and lower with control for effective results.

Dumbbell Chest fly

The dumbbell chest fly targets the chest muscles. Lie on a bench, arms extended, lower dumbbells out to the sides, then bring them back together.

Dumbbell Chest press

The dumbbell chest press is a compound exercise. Lie on a bench, press dumbbells upward, then lower them back down, engaging chest muscles for strength and growth.

Lateral Dumbbell raise

The lateral dumbbell raise targets the shoulder muscles. Stand with dumbbells at your sides, raise them out to the sides until shoulder height, then lower with control for a complete shoulder workout.

Shoulder Dumbbell press

The shoulder dumbbell press strengthens shoulder muscles. Sit or stand, press dumbbells upward until arms are straight, then lower them back down, engaging shoulders for a full-range workout.

Cable face pull

The cable face pull targets the rear deltoids and upper back. Stand facing a cable machine, pull the cable attachment towards your face, keeping elbows high and shoulder blades retracted for optimal muscle engagement.

Tricep dips

Bench tricep dips involve using a bench for support. Sit on the edge of a bench, grip the bench behind you, lower your body by bending your elbows, then push back up to target and strengthen the triceps.

Dumbell tricep extension

Dumbbell tricep extensions isolate and strengthen the triceps. Hold a dumbbell overhead with both hands, lower it behind your head by bending your elbows, then straighten your arms to return to the starting position for effective tricep activation.

Cable tricep pushdown

The cable tricep pushdown targets the triceps. Stand facing a cable machine, grip the bar attachment with an overhand grip, then push the bar downward until arms are fully extended, engaging and isolating the triceps for a complete workout.
